DirtBrownies

Description

These fun brownies are sure to give your kids a scare!

Ingredients

  • Dirt
  • Worms
  • Brownie Mix

Steps

  1. Get dirt from backyard
  2. Sift through said dirt for worms
  3. Paint your worms gummy colors
  4. Add worms to brownie mix
  5. Slap mix into a pan
  6. Profit
